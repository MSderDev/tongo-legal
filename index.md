# Privacy Policy for Tongo

**Last updated:** 2026/04/03

**Contact:** micstodev@gmail.com

## 1. Introduction

Tongo ("the App") is a multilingual vocabulary learning app. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you can control it.

We take your privacy seriously. The App is designed to work without requiring an account, login, or personal information.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

- No name, email address, or phone number
- No account or login credentials
- No precise location data
- No contacts, photos, or files
- No health or financial data

### 2.2 Crash Reports (Always Active)

We use **Firebase Crashlytics** (by Google) to collect crash reports when the app encounters an error. This helps us fix bugs and improve stability.

Data collected:
- Device model and operating system version
- App version and state at the time of the crash
- Stack trace (technical error details)
- A random installation identifier (not tied to your identity)

**Legal basis:** Legitimate interest in maintaining app stability and quality (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

### 2.3 Ads & Analytics (Only With Your Consent)

If you choose to watch ads to earn credits, we use the following services. These are **only activated after you give explicit consent** in the app:

**Google AdMob** (advertising):
- Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
- IP address (for approximate geographic targeting)
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
- Device information (model, OS, screen size)

Purpose: To display rewarded video ads and generate revenue that keeps the app free.

**Firebase Analytics** (usage analytics):
- App usage patterns (screens viewed, features used)
- Session duration and frequency
- Device model and operating system
- App version
- A random analytics identifier (not tied to your identity)

Purpose: To understand how the app is used and improve the experience.

**Legal basis:** Your explicit consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). You can grant or revoke consent at any time in Settings > Ad Consent.

## 4. Data Storage

- **On your device:** Your learning progress, credits, language preferences, and bookmarks are stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage. This data never leaves your device.
- **On third-party servers:** Crash reports, analytics data, and ad-related data are processed by Google's servers as described above.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Crash reports:** Retained by Firebase for 90 days.
- **Analytics data:** Retained by Firebase for 14 months (Google's default).
- **Ad data:** Retained according to Google's AdMob data retention policy.
- **Local data:** Stored on your device until you reset the app or uninstall it.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

- **Access** the data we hold about you
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** processing of your data
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Data portability** — receive your data in a portable format
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (Settings > Ad Consent)
- **Lodge a complaint** with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at [YOUR EMAIL].

To delete all local data, use Settings > Reset Progress or uninstall the app.

To stop crash reporting, uninstall the app. To stop analytics and ad tracking, revoke consent in Settings > Ad Consent.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
